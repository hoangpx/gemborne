Privacy Policy — GEMBORNE

Last updated: 2026-06-01 · Publisher: HPH Games · Contact: hphremotellc@gmail.com

GEMBORNE ("the App") is a single-player match-3 RPG. We respect your privacy and only collect the minimum data needed for the App to work and for the ads inside it to function.

1. Data we collect

Locally on your device (never leaves the device):

  • Game progress (current stage, HP, settings, Remove-Ads flag)

Sent to ad networks (Google AdMob) when ads are shown:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / Apple IDFA)
  • Approximate device info (model, OS version, app version, language)
  • General location at the country level (derived from IP address)
  • Whether an ad was viewed, clicked, or skipped

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, exact location, photos, contacts, microphone audio, or any account credentials.

2. Who we share data with

The only third party we share data with is Google AdMob, which serves the ads in the App. AdMob's data handling is governed by:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob policy details

Google uses the data above to choose ads, prevent fraud, measure ad performance, and (with your consent) personalise the ads you see. You can opt out of personalised ads any time in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Reset advertising ID" / "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → toggle off

3. Children's privacy

GEMBORNE is not directed at children under 13. AdMob is configured with the appropriate child-directed-treatment flags for our audience. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, email hphremotellc@gmail.com and we will delete it.

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5+ the App will ask you for tracking permission the first time you launch a game session that may show an ad. You can decline: non-personalised ads will continue to work, just at a lower revenue share for us.

5. Remove Ads (in-app purchase)

If you buy "Remove Ads", a flag stored only on your device disables all ad requests. The purchase receipt is verified through Apple App Store / Google Play Billing — those receipts are processed by Apple/Google, not by us.

6. Data retention

Game progress lives only on your device and is removed when you uninstall the App. Ad-related data is retained by Google AdMob under their policies (see link in section 2) — typically 30 days for granular event data and aggregated longer for revenue reporting.

7. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you live in the EU/UK or California you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of that data
  • Opt out of the sale of personal info (we don't sell any)

For GEMBORNE, since we don't store any personal info on our servers, "delete my data" = uninstall the App. For AdMob's retained data, use the opt-out links in section 2 or Google's privacy dashboard myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy.

8. Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last updated" date at the top whenever this policy changes. Material changes will also be announced inside the App on next launch.

9. Contact

Questions? Email hphremotellc@gmail.com.
