The Game

GEMBORNE is a single-player match-3 RPG. Swap gems, build power gems with bigger matches, unleash devastating element combos against a procession of monsters, and survive 30 stages with 6 brutal bosses. Each color (fire, water, nature, thunder, light, shadow) has unique POWER + HYPER effects.

30 StagesCrawl through 6 acts, each ending in a boss with a unique skill.
POWER & HYPERMatch 4 → POWER gem. Match 5+ → HYPER. Match them to detonate.
Streak DecayChain matches fast — your streak meter fades while idle.
SurvivalEvery match hurts the monster. The monster hits back on a timer.
