Children's Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-18 · Publisher: HPH Remote LLC · Contact: hphremotellc@gmail.com

This policy applies to HPH Remote LLC apps that are made for children (e.g. listed in Google Play's "Designed for Families" program or Apple's Kids Category). These apps are built to comply with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU GDPR (children), Google Play's Families policy, and Apple's Kids guidelines.

1. We do not collect personal information from children

Our children's apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or personally identifiable information. Specifically we do NOT collect:

  • Name, email, phone number, or address
  • Precise or coarse location
  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / Apple IDFA) or other persistent identifiers
  • Photos, camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Any account login or social profile

Game progress and settings are saved only on the device and never leave it. There is no analytics SDK that profiles the child.

2. Advertising (if the app shows ads)

If a children's app shows ads, they are served only as non-personalized, child-directed ads through a Google Play / Apple certified ads partner (Google AdMob), configured with child-directed treatment (COPPA) and "under the age of consent" flags. This means:

  • No advertising identifier is used and no behavioral profile is built.
  • Ads are contextual only and filtered to be age-appropriate.
  • No interest-based / personalized advertising is shown to children.

Some of our children's apps contain no ads at all; where that is the case, nothing in this section applies.

3. We do not share children's data

Because we do not collect personal information from children, we have nothing to sell or share. The only data leaving the device is the minimal, non-identifying request needed to fetch a contextual ad (if the app has ads), handled by AdMob under its families/COPPA settings.

4. In-app purchases & parental controls

Any purchases (e.g. "Remove Ads") are processed entirely by Apple App Store / Google Play Billing — we never see payment details. We recommend parents enable purchase protection: Google Play → require authentication for purchases; Apple → Screen Time → Content & Privacy → require Ask to Buy.

5. Parental rights

Since we store no personal data about your child, there is nothing for us to retrieve or delete from a server — uninstalling the app removes all on-device data. If you have any question or concern about your child's privacy, email hphremotellc@gmail.com and we will respond promptly.

6. Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last updated" date above whenever this policy changes and, for material changes, note them inside the app on next launch.

7. Contact

Questions about our children's apps? Email hphremotellc@gmail.com.
